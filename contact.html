Common Questions

How do I cancel my ticket? ▼

Log in to your account and go to "My Bookings". Select the booking and click "Cancel". Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days if cancelled 2+ hours before departure.

Can I change my travel date? ▼

Yes, date changes are allowed up to 24 hours before departure. A rescheduling fee of LKR 100 applies. Contact us or use the self-service portal.

Is my e-ticket accepted at the station? ▼

Yes. Show the e-ticket QR code on your phone or a printout to the ticket inspector. No physical ticket collection is needed.
